“Memory” is a short story written by H. P. Lovecraft, was first published in 1923 in The National Amateur, and is believed to be in the public domain. Digital scans can be found at no known repository. A transcription was produced by "unnamed volunteers" and transcripts can be found at Wikisource.

The work was translated from English to Esperanto as “Rememoro” in 2023 by "a pseudonymous translator," this translation was revised in 2024 by Paul King and that translation is released under the CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) Public Domain Dedication.

The cover page was adapted from Phayre's Leaf Monkey Thinking by Tareq Aziz Touhid, and that image was released under the Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International Deed (CC BY-SA 4.0).
